Melinda Gates

Empowering Women

Bill and Melinda started the Bill and Melinda gates Foundation in 2000, which includes a global Gender Equality strategy. The primary goal is to remove the barrriers facing women and girls to ensure they have an equal chance to thrive and lead healthy, productive lives

Melinda Gates started Pivotal ventures in 2015 as an investment and incubation company working to drive social progress for women and families in the United States

On October 5,2019 Gates announced in an article she wrote for Time Magazine that her investment company Pivotal Ventures will donate $1 billion to gender quality

Melinda launched the website Evoke in March ,2019 as place to highlight innovative ideas and creative solutions solutions from people who are committed to improving the world.The launched during Women's History Month, which was no coincidence according to Gates
